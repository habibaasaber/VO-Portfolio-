About

Voice-Over Artist | Dubbing & Narration

I am a passionate Voice-Over Artist with a professional career active since 2019. My vocal journey has led me to explore a diverse range of disciplines, from the emotional depth of film dubbing to the precise clarity of educational narration.

With a warm and expressive tone, I specialize in storytelling and character voices, bringing narratives to life with authenticity. My experience spans radio presenting, social media content, and high-profile AI voice cloning projects. I operate from my professional home studio, ensuring every project is delivered with top-tier audio quality.
